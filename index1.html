ABOUT NAYA LABS

THE ORIGIN STORY

Naya Labs began with a like-minded duo and a group of best friends who realized that success isn't just about hard work—it's about the right strategy. We started as a Data-as-a-Service (DaaS) venture, providing career target data to job seekers.


But while analyzing the numbers, we found a critical gap: our users had the right data to find jobs, but lacked the personal branding to land them. That insight drove our transformation into Naya Labs. Today, we combine our data-driven roots with creative storytelling, having successfully optimized 208 resumes, revamped 96 LinkedIn profiles, and deployed 50+ portfolio websites. We don't just find opportunities; we make you ready for them.

FLAT HIERARCHY PROTOCOL

We rejected the corporate ladder. Naya Labs operates on a Flat Grid Structure. Led by our CEO, the flow of ideas is bidirectional.


Whether you are a Lead, a Trainee, or a Campus Ambassador, your input goes directly to the top. We don't just build careers for others; we build leaders internally.
